Project Design Report (PDR)
Financial Risk Analysis & Credit Default Prediction System
1. Problem Statement

Financial institutions such as banks, NBFCs, and fintech lenders face significant losses due to credit defaults, where borrowers fail to repay loans on time or at all. Traditional credit evaluation methods rely heavily on static rules, limited financial indicators, and manual judgment, which often fail to capture complex customer behavior and evolving risk patterns.

As a result:

High-risk customers may receive loans.

Low-risk customers may be unfairly rejected.

Institutions suffer increased Non-Performing Assets (NPAs) and revenue loss.

There is a need for a data-driven, predictive system that can accurately assess credit risk and forecast default probability using historical financial and customer data.

2. Why This Problem Is Worth Solving
Business Impact

Reduces financial losses from loan defaults.

Improves profitability through better credit decisions.

Enhances portfolio risk management.

Social Impact

Enables fairer lending decisions.

3. Root Causes Analysis
Root Cause	Explanation
Manual risk assessment	Human bias and inconsistent judgment
Limited rule-based models	Cannot adapt to changing patterns
Poor feature utilization	Underuse of customer behavior data
Lack of predictive analytics	Decisions based on past defaults only
Weak validation practices	Models not properly evaluated

These issues persist due to legacy systems and lack of advanced analytics adoption.

4. Proposed Solution Overview

The proposed system is a Financial Risk Analysis & Credit Default Prediction Platform that uses statistical analysis and machine learning models to predict whether a customer is likely to default.

Key Capabilities:

Analyze financial, demographic, and behavioral data

Identify key credit risk indicators

Predict default probability

Provide explainable risk insights

Why This Solution Is Better

Continuously improvable

Objective and consistent decision-making

Supports regulatory compliance via documentation
